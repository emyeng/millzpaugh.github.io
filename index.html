Projects

Uncovering Student Debt

Wondering why everyone you know has student loans? This project overviews the national student debt since 2002 by analyzing datasets from the Department of Education.

Radar

If you're looking for a job (like most of General Assembly's Immersive students), there are probably days when you don't want to campus to view available jobs from partner organizations. This project digitizes the GA job board so students and employers can connect and collaborate outside of the office!

Adopt.me

Adopt.me offers new ways to look at old pets! Using the Petfinder database, the site allows users to view animals up for adoption in New York City. You can add pets to your favorites, vote up an adorable cat or pup, and maybe even take one home with you!
